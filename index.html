Welcome to GDG OnCampus
Who Are We? GDG PESCE is a dynamic community based at P.E.S. College of Engineering, dedicated to bringing students and professionals together to explore the latest advancements in technology. As part of the global Google Developer Groups initiative, we aim to inspire, educate, and connect individuals passionate about technology. What Do We Do?
We organize a variety of events to foster learning, collaboration, and innovation, including:
Workshops: Hands-on sessions covering tools and technologies like Google Cloud, Generative AI, and App Development. Seminars: Expert talks and knowledge-sharing platforms. Community Engagement: Activities to encourage collaboration among members.
Our Focus
At GDG PESCE, we emphasize empowering members with skills in:
Google Cloud: Building scalable and efficient cloud solutions. Generative AI: Exploring AI-powered innovations. Web Development: Crafting modern, responsive, and dynamic websites. App Development: Creating mobile applications for Android and iOS. Our Vision
To create a collaborative environment that drives innovation, strengthens our community, and contributes to a better tech ecosystem by: Organizing larger, impactful events. Expanding our membership base. Increasing active participation in all initiatives.
